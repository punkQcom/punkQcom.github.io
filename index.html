VST Collaboration

Share files directly inside your DAW. Simple sign-in, fast uploads, and clear folder structure.

About

VST Collaboration is a lightweight plugin that makes it easy to upload, organize, and share project files without leaving your creative workflow.

Features

Secure sign-in with Google, Microsoft, and GitHub.

Drag-and-drop uploads, folder management, and clear file lists.

Designed for music creators who collaborate across DAWs.
