Common questions

When does the beta end? + −

Beta runs through Q3 2026. Early beta users get locked-in pricing and early access to all new products in the Q-Suite.

Is my code sent to your servers? + −

By default, punkQ runs analysis locally. When you opt into cloud features, only the specific context needed is sent — never your full codebase unless you explicitly share it.

What editors do you support? + −

VS Code and JetBrains IDEs are in beta. Neovim plugin and CLI are in alpha. Zed support is planned for Q4 2026.
