VST Collaboration

Share project files directly inside your DAW

VST Collaboration is a VST3 plugin that lets you upload, manage, and share project files without leaving your DAW. Sign in with Google, GitHub, Microsoft, or Discord. Invite collaborators to your projects, and share files instantly with QuickShare links.


Features

  • Files — Upload, download, and organise files in your Personal and Shared storage
  • QuickShare — Create temporary web links to share folders with anyone
  • Projects — Invite collaborators by email to shared project folders
