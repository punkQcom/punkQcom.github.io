Privacy Policy


This privacy policy explains how VST Collaboration ("we", "us") handles information when you use our plugin and related services.


Information We Collect

We collect basic account information during sign-in, such as your name, email address, and profile picture, depending on the provider you choose (Google, Microsoft, GitHub).

When you use the plugin, we process file metadata (file names, sizes, and paths) and the files you upload or share.


How We Use Information

We use your account information to authenticate you and to show your identity inside the app.

We use your file data to upload, list, and share content with people you choose.


Storage and Security

Files are stored in cloud storage. We take reasonable steps to secure data in transit and at rest.


Sharing

We do not sell your data. We only share data with your chosen collaborators and with service providers required to operate the service.


Retention

We keep your account and file data as long as your account is active, or as needed to provide the service. You can delete files or request account removal.
