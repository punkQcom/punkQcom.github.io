Try It Free

The Free plan is fully functional with no time limit and no credit card required. Upload files, create a project, invite a collaborator, and share a QuickShare link — all the core features work on the Free plan. Upgrade only when you need more storage or collaboration capacity.


How Subscriptions Work

Download the plugin, sign in, and start using it for free. When you're ready for more storage or collaboration features, upgrade from the Account tab inside the plugin. Payments are handled securely by our payment provider.


Cancellation & Refunds

You can cancel your subscription at any time from inside the plugin. Your paid features remain active until the end of the current billing period. After that, your account reverts to the Free plan — your files stay intact.

If you're not satisfied, contact support@punkq.com within 14 days of your first payment for a full refund.
